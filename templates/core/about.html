About TEX

Tool Exchange

Do you have tools that you arent always using? Why not make some extra money with them. Its as simple as posting your tools on TEX. You set your price, agree on a date and time, and get paid.


What if my tools get damaged you ask? No need to worry, we will hold a deposit from the renter until the tool is returned and you confirm the condition. We do ask both the rentee and renter take photos and document the condition before and after for legal security.
